Frequently Asked Questions

What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is the #1 AI CRM (customer relationship management) platform. It helps companies connect sales, service, marketing, commerce, and IT with a single, integrated view of the customer powered by data and agentic AI. Salesforce enables teams to close deals faster, improve customer service, and drive smarter business decisions.
What is CRM and why do I need one?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. A CRM system helps you manage customer interactions, track sales opportunities, automate workflows, and improve customer relationships. Businesses use CRM to stay organized, increase revenue, and deliver better customer experiences.
What is agentic AI and how is it used in Salesforce?
Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can reason, take actions, and work autonomously across tasks. In Salesforce, agentic AI helps automate workflows, generate insights, assist agents and sellers, and personalize customer interactions while keeping humans in control.
How does Salesforce CRM help my small business grow?
Salesforce CRM helps small businesses grow by organizing customer data, automating follow-ups, tracking leads, and providing insights into sales performance. This allows teams to focus on building relationships, closing more deals, and scaling efficiently as the business grows.
