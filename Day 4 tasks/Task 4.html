History of College

Published on November 07, 2025 by Murali jay

The history of Aditya College dates back to its inception in 1984 by N.S Reddy, who aimed to create a platform for holistic growth and success for students at all levels.

The college has since grown into a network of educational institutions, including Junior Colleges, Degree Colleges, PG Colleges, Engineering Colleges, Pharmacy Colleges, Nursing Colleges, and Teacher Training Institutions.

Aditya College has been recognized for its commitment to quality education and has achieved numerous accolades, including being a springboard for success through its vision, innovation, and professional excellence.

The college has a rich standing in the educational era and has been involved in various initiatives to promote engineering excellence and provide students with a globally competitive and employable education.
