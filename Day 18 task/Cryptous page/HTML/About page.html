About Cryptous

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange. Unlike traditional currencies issued by governments (fiat), cryptocurrencies are decentralized and typically not controlled by any central authority, such as a bank. The first and most well-known cryptocurrency is Bitcoin, created in 2009 by an anonymous person or group known as Satoshi Nakamoto. As of now, there are over 25,000 different cryptocurrencies available in the market, with varying functionalities and purposes.
